      ABOUT / 01 — THE BAND

      THE BAND

      Slow Erosion is a metalcore band from Wuhan, China, formed in 2023. Rooted in the intensity of metalcore and shaped by elements of black metal, post-rock, and shoegaze, the band builds a sound that moves between violence and dream, oppression and weightlessness.

      The name "Slow Erosion" reflects a gradual form of collapse within modern life — the way agency, emotion, resistance, and perception are worn down not in a single moment, but through constant pressure from systems, technology, capital, and social order. Rather than focusing only on anger or confrontation, Slow Erosion traces the quieter and more invisible wounds of contemporary existence: numbness, alienation, spiritual exhaustion, and the feeling of being slowly reshaped by forces beyond one's control.

      Through crushing riffs, blackened textures, atmospheric passages, and immersive layers of noise, Slow Erosion creates music that is both aggressive and fragile. Their sound captures the tension between rage and surrender, clarity and dissociation, human resistance and systemic decay.
